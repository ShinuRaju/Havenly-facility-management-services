About Us

GtS Facility Management Services Pvt Ltd was created in the year 2015, with a vision to provide customers in India with tailor made solutions with sustainable focus on quality and customer satisfaction that matches global standards. Our commitment revolves around this philosophy and it is this commitment that has made GtS has one of the fast emerging organisation in the Facility Management Industry in India.

Equipped with necessary infrastructure and resources, GtS’s capability to offer high end services across verticals encompassing commercial, retail, residential, hospitality and Industrial sectors have been exceptional. GtS in a short span of time has emerged successful in providing comprehensive outsourcing and support services in all sectors of the economy.

Our Team

Each member of the team is suitably recruited for the job put through a planned training routine in our training centre, and deployed on the field with the necessary tools to succeed. Our teams take clear ownership of service delivery and look to work closely with our clients to ensure our approach to service delivery is unified and it’s as per the client’s requirement with optimised costs.
